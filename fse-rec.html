FSE-REC Project



Project Overview

For my 6 week experiential learning module at the end of my second university a team of four other students and I were tasked with creating a file upload control system for the University of Chester Faculty of Science and Engineering Research Ethics Committee. This site needed to provide the functionality for students to upload their research request forms, and for staff/ committee members to view these forms, make comments on them and approve/deny the request. My Role was project leader and back-end programming. I was responsible for arranging and conducting interviews with the client, and relaying the information collected back to my team. I was also responsible for the back-end programming of the website, responsible for providing the functionality of the site.
