Project Overview

For my 6 week experiential learning module at the end of my second university a team of four other students and I were tasked with creating a file upload control system for the University of Chester Faculty of Science and Engineering Research Ethics Committee. This site needed to provide the functionality for students to upload their research request forms and be able to view their uploads and control them (e.g. delete them), and for staff/ committee members to view these forms, make comments on them and approve/deny the request.
My Role was project leader and back-end programming. I was responsible for arranging and conducting interviews with the client, and relaying the information collected back to my team. I was also responsible for the back-end programming of the website, responsible for providing the functionality of the site.

Skills Developed
  • Enhanced my knowledge of C# programming language and back-end web programming
  • Further Development of ASP.NET and putting current knowledge into practice for a client based project
  • Developed team working & communication skills, using slack as our primary method of communication if we were not all in the same work space
  • Gained experience in managing a project, delegating roles & responsibilities to other team members and ensuring constant progress was made and deadlines were met
  • Put existing knowledge of SQL statements into practice, using them to connect to a database, store user submitted data and retrieve existing data
  • Gained experience in using source control within a team project. We used gitlab for source control.
